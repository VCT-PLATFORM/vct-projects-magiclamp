# PATTERN: Client Onboarding Golden Path — Giảm time-to-first-delivery 60%

## Pattern Type: Strategic
## Applicable To: ml_account, brand_architect, creative_ai, ml_legal

## Problem
Client mới sign contract → đội production mất 2-3 tuần để deliver content đầu tiên. Client mất kiên nhẫn.

## Solution: 5-Day Golden Path
| Day | Action | Owner |
|-----|--------|-------|
| D1 | Contract signed + Brand DNA Workshop (2h call) | ml_account + brand_architect |
| D2 | Brand DNA document hoàn thành + Research kick-off | brand_architect + market_researcher |
| D3 | Creative Brief + 3 script drafts | creative_ai |
| D4 | Visual assets + Client review round 1 | visual_generator + ml_account |
| D5 | Revision + First deliverable SHIPPED | editor_assistant + ml_account |

## Key Principles
1. **Parallel execution**: Research và Creative chạy song song, không tuần tự.
2. **Brand DNA Workshop**: Thu thập 80% info cần thiết trong 1 buổi, không email qua lại.
3. **First Deliverable = Quick Win**: Ship 1 content đơn giản trước (Instagram post, TikTok 15s) thay vì đợi full campaign.
